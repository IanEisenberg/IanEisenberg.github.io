Ian W Eisenberg

AI · Neuroscience · Art · Play

Hi! I'm a cognitive neuroscientist now working on AI. I focus on system-level interventions to make AI more effective and beneficial. To that end, I lead Credo AI's Data Science team, where I help organizations govern their AI systems responsibly.

My perspective draws from the social and life sciences, my experience as a machine learning engineer, and my involvement in the broader AI safety community. My overriding motivation is to use my career to solve challenging problems facing humanity and improve the likelihood that our technologies support our flourishing.

Outside of these serious things, I value play, exploration and creativity. My hobbies and the communities around me enrich and enliven life.
